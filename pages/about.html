About Us

Introduction

Sne Mshengu is a dedicated educational Psychologist with a strong academic foundation, holding a Bachelor of Arts (Wits), Bachelor of Social Science Honours, and a Master’s in Educational Psychology (UKZN).

She is committed to providing compassionate, evidence-based support to clients dealing with anxiety, depression, stress, and personal growth challenges.

Sne’s approach emphasizes empathy, active listening, and practical strategies like Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT).

Her expertise in educational psychology and emotional counseling helps clients build resilience, achieve personal goals, and navigate life’s challenges with confidence. Whether you’re seeking support for specific issues or personal development, Sne provides a safe, welcoming space to foster growth and healing.
